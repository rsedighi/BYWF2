    BEAM History

    The idea for BEAM Your Workforce Benefit has its origins in 2018, under the umbrella of New Orleans Workforce Innovations (NOWI), a resource navigation pilot of the Greater New Orleans Foundation (GNOF). The primary objective of NOWI was to provide post-hire support to local businesses by anticipating and addressing various barriers faced by their workforce. The program specifically targeted industries such as hospitality, construction, and healthcare in the Greater New Orleans area.

    ​

    From its inception, NOWI's efforts succeeded in achieving its mission. The program played a particularly crucial role in supporting businesses during the challenging times brought about by the COVID pandemic. By offering assistance and resources, NOWI helped bolster the resilience of vulnerable businesses and their workforces.

    Powell DiGangi, one of the founding navigators of the NOWI program, has now created BEAM based on his work with the Foundation. Kellie Chavez Greene, Vice President for Programs, recently noted, “BEAM has established its own identity and we are grateful Powell is continuing to lead in the area of workforce resource navigation for our region. We know resource navigation is a critical component to a successful workforce ecosystem.” 

     

    BEAM strives to continuously enhance and expand its services to better serve its employer partners, their workforces, and the communities in which they operate.

     

    Through its work, BEAM addresses a wide range of barriers faced by the workforce, fostering a more supportive and inclusive workplace environment.  By doing so, the program contributes to the betterment of local businesses, promotes economic growth, and builds stronger workforce bonds and satisfaction.
